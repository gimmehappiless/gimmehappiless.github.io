    Our Brand

    Our Goal

Our brand is not constrained by traditional and is open to trial. We hope to bring happiness to all. Hence, the existed and boring fonts are never our options when designing the typography of our logo. The geometry of the font and the visualized design made our font unique and modern. With every stroke bold and wide, the font itself brings a heavy impact on the viewers. But still, the arc and the simplified style avoid the heaviness of the font and give it a sense of youth. Those geometry patterns energetically surrounding our logo, “Gimme Happiness, ” not only makes people naturally think of the buttons on the DualShock but also brings harmony and rhythm to our logo.
